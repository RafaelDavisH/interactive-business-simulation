Key Customer Discovery Principles
You've completed the core customer discovery activities. The skills you practiced here (listening over pitching, surfacing workarounds, and mapping insights to themes) are what separate companies that build what customers want from those that build what founders assume customers want.
🎙️
Interview quality matters more than quantity. 5 deep, well-structured interviews beat 50 surveys. Past behavior reveals truth; future intent does not.
👤
Early adopters are not your whole market. They're the 2–5% with the problem so acute they'll tolerate an imperfect solution. Build for them first.
🗂️
Affinity mapping reveals the signal. When multiple customers independently express the same theme, you've found a pattern worth designing around.
